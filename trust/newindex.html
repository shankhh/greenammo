Empowering People. Enabling Progress. Enriching the Planet.

GreenAmmo is a Trust working towards environmental protection, sustainable development, community engagement/empowerment and infrastructure development. We are a group of individuals who have come together to form this Trust because we realized we have crossed the point of no-return. Now is the time to act towards giving back to this beautiful nature that sustains every breath of our existence. So we venture out to various vulnerable ecological, economical and geographical locations—from mountains, to sea, to Ghats—and raise awareness about waste management and build self-sustaining waste management systems that can be run by the locals themselves, thus ensuring its sustainability. Nature knows no boundaries, neither do we! Waste knows no domain, neither do we.
