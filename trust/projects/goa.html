Project Milestones

Goa

Building an eco-retreat from coastal waste in South Goa, turning marine pollution into sustainable accommodation.

  • Site Transformation – Converting a barren plot into an eco-friendly retreat for nature lovers:
    • Structures from decommissioned buses and shipping containers.
    • Dedicated BottleBrick construction segment.
  • Material Sourcing – Conducted clean-ups at Arambol, Anjuna, Baga, Morjim, Calangute, and other North Goa beaches.
  • Waste Integration – BottleBricks made during clean-ups to be directly used in the eco-accommodation build.
