Explore Our Initiatives

GreenAmmo is a Trust working towards environmental protection, sustainable development, community engagement/empowerment, and infrastructure development. We venture out to various vulnerable ecological, economical, and geographical locations—from mountains, to sea, to Ghats—and raise awareness about **waste management** and build **self-sustaining waste management systems** that can be run by the locals themselves, thus ensuring its sustainability. Nature knows no boundaries, neither do we! Waste knows no domain, neither do we.
